About

Understanding what
others cannot see

Anxiety disorders are the most common mental health challenge among students today. Yet for those who have never experienced it, anxiety remains abstract. A word, not a feeling.

If You Were Me is a short AI-generated film that places you inside the experience of a young person living with social anxiety. Through everyday moments, a morning alarm, a crowded hallway, a classroom, the film reveals what it feels like when anxiety is always present, even when no one around you can tell.

Created by students at Tallinn University, grounded in psychological research, and guided by expert feedback.
